Accessibility Features



Dysgraphia

Transform your spoken thoughts into written ideas effortlessly. This feature listens to your speech, simplifies it, and offers three potential prompts that match your intended meaning. Choose the best fit or refine further, and seamlessly send the output into the chat.

Dyslexia

Experience enhanced reading accessibility with text-to-speech conversion. The feature reads text aloud and provides a simplified version for easier understanding, helping you grasp the core message effortlessly.

Dyspraxia

Turn your handwriting into text, by uploading an image of your handwritten notes. The feature will interpret and convert it into clear, digital text, ready for use or sharing.

Autism

Simplify communication with a chat assistant designed for clarity. It translates complex language, such as slang or idioms, into straightforward expressions, making conversations more accessible and easy to understand.

AI Chat

Interact with a versatile AI assistant for all your needs. Whether it's information, advice, or creative ideas, this feature is here to provide natural and intuitive assistance.
