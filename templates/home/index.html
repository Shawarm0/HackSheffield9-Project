Accessibility Features

Dysgraphia

Easily transform your spoken thoughts into written ideas. The feature listens to your speech, simplifies it, and offers three potential prompts that align with your intended meaning. Choose the most fitting option, or request the model to refine your ideas further. Once satisfied, seamlessly send the output into the chat.

Dyslexia

Enhance reading accessibility with text-to-speech conversion. This feature reads text aloud while also presenting a simplified version for easier understanding, helping you grasp the core message without unnecessary complexity.

Dyspraxia

Turn your handwriting into editable text effortlessly. Upload an image of your handwritten notes, and the feature will accurately interpret and convert it into clear, digital text, ready for further use or sharing.

Dyscalculia

Coming soon: Tools and features to assist with numerical understanding and calculations.

Autism

Simplify communication with a chat assistant designed for clarity. It translates complex language, such as slang or idioms, into straightforward expressions, making conversations more accessible and easier to understand.

AI Chat

Engage with a versatile AI assistant for all your queries and needs. Whether you're seeking information, advice, or creative ideas, this general-purpose chat is here to help in a natural and intuitive way.
